Who Is Orijin Plus ?

We are two West Australia brothers, James and Rhys, founders and operators of Origin Plus. We have been involved in international markets and exporting through our whole careers and in every venture we have maintained a passion for staying ahead of the curve and developing leading technologies to help outperform our competitors that often stay lazily dormant.

At Orijin Plus we are exporters at heart, we have intricate understanding of the challenges you face in your field and we strongly believe this sets us apart from other traditional tech companies.

We originally built Orijin Plus to eradicate counterfeiting of our products and to maintain our competitive edge, as we exported to one of the hardest markets in the world and one of its most competitive F&B product categories.
